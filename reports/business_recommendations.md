# Business Recommendations

## Recommendation 1: Monitor Late Delivery Rate as a Core KPI

Late delivery rate should be tracked as a core fulfilment KPI because the dashboard shows a clear difference in review score between late and on-time deliveries.

### Suggested action

Create a weekly fulfilment report showing:

- Total delivered orders
- Late delivered orders
- Late delivery rate
- Average delivery days
- Average delay days
- Review score by delivery status

---

## Recommendation 2: Investigate High-Risk Customer States

Some customer states show higher late delivery rates and longer delivery times.

### Suggested action

A fulfilment team should review:

- Regional carrier performance
- Distance from seller regions
- Delivery lead time assumptions
- Estimated delivery date accuracy
- Local logistics constraints

---

## Recommendation 3: Review High-Freight Product Categories

Some product categories show higher average freight values.

### Suggested action

The business should review:

- Product weight and dimensions
- Packaging requirements
- Carrier pricing
- Freight cost recovery
- Product margin impact

---

## Recommendation 4: Analyse Seller Geography

Seller activity appears concentrated in certain seller states.

### Suggested action

The business should review whether seller location affects:

- Delivery speed
- Late delivery rate
- Freight cost
- Customer review score
- Fulfilment risk

---

## Recommendation 5: Use Customer Review Score as a Service Quality Signal

Review score should be used alongside delivery metrics because it reflects the customer experience impact of fulfilment performance.

### Suggested action

Track review score by:

- Delivery status
- Product category
- Customer state
- Seller state
- Delay band

---

## Recommendation 6: Build a Late Delivery Risk Model

A future version of this project could use machine learning to predict late delivery risk.

### Possible model inputs

- Customer state
- Seller state
- Product category
- Freight value
- Item value
- Estimated delivery days
- Product weight
- Product dimensions

### Business use

The model could help flag high-risk orders before they become late, allowing proactive intervention.
